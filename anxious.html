You Crave Connection — But Fear Losing It

Anxious attachers often feel deeply about relationships. You love big, give a lot, but constantly wonder, "Will they leave me?"

Protest Behaviors

  • 🔺 Texting or calling repeatedly when anxious
  • 🔺 Acting distant to get attention
  • 🔺 Creating conflict to provoke reassurance
  • 🔺 Stalking social media or checking ‘last seen’
  • 🔺 Over-apologizing even when not at fault

What You Might Say

“I feel like you’re slipping away, and I don’t know what I did wrong.”

Attachment Matchups: How It Feels

  • 💬 Anxious + Anxious: Deep bonding, but risk of overthinking and emotional flooding.
  • 💬 Anxious + Avoidant: A frustrating dance of closeness and withdrawal — highs and lows.
  • 💬 Anxious + Secure: A healing space where you feel safe, seen, and grounded.

Awareness is your superpower. Healing begins when you notice the fear beneath the behavior.
