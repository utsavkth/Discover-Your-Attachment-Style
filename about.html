The Personal Journey Behind This Project

Like many people, I’ve often found myself asking: “Why do I behave the way I do in relationships?” Why do I sometimes get too attached? Or pull away when things get real?

My turning point came when I read Attached by Amir Levine and Rachel Heller. That book didn’t just explain my patterns — it gave me language for what I’d been feeling all along.

That insight planted the seed for this project. I wanted to build something accessible, insightful, and even a little fun — something that helps you see yourself with compassion.

This quiz isn’t about labeling or diagnosing. It’s about awareness — a mirror to reflect how you connect, how you love, and how your early emotional wiring might still shape your relationships today.

My hope is that it empowers you the way it empowered me — to move toward healthier relationships, with others and with yourself.

From one curious human to another:
Welcome to your emotional blueprint.

— Utsav
